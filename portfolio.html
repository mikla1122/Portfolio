Candy Crush

Tank Raid

Visit Candy Crush
Description:

"Candy Crush" a "match three" game, where the core gameplay is based on swapping two adjacent candies among several on the gameboard to make a row or column of at least three matching-colored candies. On this match, the matched candies are removed from the board, and candies above them fall into the empty spaces, with new candies appearing from the top of the board. This may create a new matched set of candies, which is automatically cleared in the same manner.

Role in the Project:

I was the lead developer, responsible for designing and coding the tank movement mechanics, collision detection, and physics simulations. I also developed the power-up system and integrated an upgrade mechanism, allowing players to improve their tanks as they progress. I worked on optimizing the game for better performance, ensuring smooth gameplay even on lower-end devices.

Tools/Technologies Used:

Unity, C#, Blender, Adobe Illustrator, GitHub
